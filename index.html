About Our Legacy

A Heritage of Textile Excellence

Arzoo Textile Mills Ltd. was founded by Haji Abdul Majeed Sheikh, who migrated from Calcutta in the 1930s and established a textile trading business. Over the decades, the Sheikh family expanded operations with offices in New York, London, and Italy, evolving into a fully integrated textile manufacturer and exporter based in Faisalabad, Pakistan.
