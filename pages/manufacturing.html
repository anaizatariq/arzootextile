Our Facilities

8 Integrated Production Stages

Weaving

State-of-the-art weaving looms producing a wide range of fabric constructions with precision and consistency.

Bleaching Plant

Modern bleaching facility ensuring uniform whiteness and fabric preparation for subsequent processing stages.

03

Advanced mercerizing equipment that enhances fabric luster, strength, and dye affinity for superior finish.

High-precision rotary and flat bed printing capabilities for intricate designs and patterns on diverse fabric types.

Comprehensive dyeing facilities with accurate color matching and reproduction for consistent quality across production runs.

CAD & Engraving

Computer-aided design and engraving department for creating and preparing print designs with high accuracy.

Stitching Unit

Fully equipped stitching unit for manufacturing finished textile products including bed linen, curtains, and accessories.

08

Industrial quilting machines for producing quilted fabrics and finished quilted products to international specifications.
