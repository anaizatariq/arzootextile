Our Products

Textile Products for Global Markets

Explore our comprehensive range of premium textile products manufactured to international standards.

Woven Fabric

Our woven fabric division produces a comprehensive range of premium quality fabrics suitable for apparel, home furnishing, and industrial applications. We utilize advanced weaving technology to deliver fabrics with exceptional consistency, durability, and finish.

Home Bed Linen

Our home bed linen collection includes duvet covers, bed sheets, pillow cases, and complete bedding sets manufactured to the highest international standards. Available in a wide range of designs, thread counts, and fabric compositions to meet diverse market requirements.

Institutional Textile

We supply durable, high-performance textiles for hospitals, hotels, and institutional use. Our institutional textile range is designed to withstand heavy industrial laundering while maintaining quality and appearance over extended use.

Bath Linen

Our bath linen collection features premium bath towels, hand towels, face towels, and bath mats. Manufactured using high-quality cotton with superior absorbency and softness, our bath linens meet the exacting standards of international buyers.

Curtains

We offer an extensive range of curtain fabrics and ready-made curtains in various styles, textures, and designs. Our curtain collection includes sheer, blackout, and decorative options suitable for residential and commercial applications.

Kitchen Accessories

Our kitchen accessories range includes aprons, oven mitts, pot holders, tea towels, and table linen. Each product is crafted with attention to functionality, durability, and aesthetic appeal for global retail and hospitality markets.
